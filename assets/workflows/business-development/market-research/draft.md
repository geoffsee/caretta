## Instructions
Produce a comprehensive market research report with these sections:
### 1. Market Landscape
- Define the current market size and growth trajectory for {{project_name}}.
- Identify the key market segments and their primary needs/pain points.
- Note any macroeconomic trends, regulatory shifts, or technological changes impacting the market.
### 2. Target Audience & Personas
- Define the ideal customer profile (ICP) for the current stage of the project.
- Detail 2-3 key personas, including their goals, challenges, and where they hang out (online/offline).
- Identify the primary value proposition of {{project_name}} for each persona.
### 3. Opportunity Analysis
- Identify "whitespace" in the market where current solutions are failing.
- Highlight potential geographic or vertical-specific opportunities for expansion.
- List 3-5 high-potential segments that {{project_name}} should target next.
### 4. Growth Channels
- Analyze the most effective channels for reaching the target audience (e.g., Content, SEO, Paid, Community, Direct Sales).
- Evaluate the potential ROI and effort for each channel.
- Recommend a prioritized list of 3 experiments to run in the next month.
### 5. Strategic Recommendations
- Summarize the top 3-5 strategic priorities for business development based on this research.
- Provide a clear rationale for each recommendation, linked back to market data or trends.
## Format
Keep the report factual and data-driven where possible. Use clear headings and bullet points for readability.
This is a DRAFT for human review. The human will provide feedback, correct assumptions, and help prioritize the recommendations.
